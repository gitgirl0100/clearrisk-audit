Help & Information

How to Use:

Enter a token smart contract address and click "Evaluate Risk" to generate an audit report.

Understanding Results:

The system calculates a risk score based on ownership, liquidity, distribution, code transparency, and activity indicators.

Blockchain Logging:

When enabled, audit results are recorded on the Ethereum Sepolia test network for transparency and verification.
